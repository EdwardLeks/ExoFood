Description

This app will notify you when your food is starting to expire. You can enter or scan the date of expiring or manually search for the food item in our database if you don't know the date of expiring. You can set your notification to alert you any time before the food expires. It will solve the problem of very long food keeping, alerting you when your food is starting to go bad.
